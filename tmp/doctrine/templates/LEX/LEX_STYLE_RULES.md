## Tone & Intent
- Calm, clear, sincere; no hype or flattery.
- Subtle confidence; authenticity over persuasion.

## Rhythm
- Short paragraphs; varied sentences; whitespace for cadence.

## Punctuation
- Em dashes sparingly; use `---` only.

## Markdown Hygiene
- Semantic headings; list hierarchy; quotes used properly.

## Medium Variants (Operational §4)
- Pattern: structured, analytical, dry.
- Podcast: reflective narration + whitespace.
- LinkedIn: warm, short paragraphs, reflective end.
- Essay: longform, emotionally grounded, high signal.

## Anti-Patterns
- Avoid “best practice” framing; avoid motivational tropes.
